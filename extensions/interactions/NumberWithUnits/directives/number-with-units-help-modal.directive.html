Guidelines for writing units

Rules for writing units

Rule Description Incorrect Format Correct Format
Separate the value and the unit with one space. 3km 3 km
If the value is a fraction, separate numerator and denominator by a forward slash '/' symbol. 3 by 5 km 3/5 km
For multiplying two or more units, put one space or asterisk '*' between them. 5 J-s
5 J . s		 5 J s
5 J*s
Separate numerator and denominator of the units using '/' or 'per' symbol. 5 kmperhr 5 km/hr
5 km per hr
For adding dimensions to the unit, use the caret '^' symbol to add both positive and negative powers. 5 kg / m(2)
5 kg / m**2
5 kg m^(-2)		 5 kg / m^2
5 kg / m^2
5 kg m^-2
In case of multiple units in the denominator, enclose them within the parenthesis. 5 kg / m * s
5 kg/m*s		 5 kg / (m * s)
5 kg / m / s
