Get involved!

Oppia is an open commons for education. And, true to this ethos, it is an open-source website created through the hard work and creativity of people like you! To get involved in the Oppia project and help us bring about our mission of free, high-quality universal education, reach out via our oppia-dev@ Google Group, or contact us at admin@oppia.org. We're excited to hear from you!

Also, if you have any feedback for us, please don't hesitate to let us know via our feedback form — we read all the feedback we receive. Thanks in advance!

For all other enquiries, please use one of the following forums or email addresses:

  • General questions about the site: please post your question to the Oppia forum.

  • Security issues: please contact admin@oppia.org.

  • Press enquiries: please contact press@oppia.org.

  • Partnership enquiries: please contact partnerships@oppia.org.
    We're particularly interested in meeting partners who can help bring lessons to a large audience, or who'd like to create lessons on topics that students find difficult. If you're an individual creator, you might also be interested in our Teach with Oppia program.

  • All other enquiries: please contact admin@oppia.org.
