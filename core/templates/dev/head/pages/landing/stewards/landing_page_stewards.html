Free, effective lessons you can use at home

With Oppia, you can help your child study at home with the best resources available, and you can guide them without needing to be an expert in the subject. Oppia's free, effective lessons teach students the skills they’ll need to succeed academically and in later life.

Spend quality time with your child and personally connect with them as you guide them through lessons in an interactive and fun learning environment.

Oppia is provided as a service to the community, and the lessons are free to use. You can join our community to support the project, have a say in what your child is being taught, and give us feedback on how the lessons could be developed to suit your child's needs.

Free, personalized lessons that your students can use to learn

With Oppia, you can get access to additional free online lessons to supplement your classroom teaching. Help your students by introducing them to Oppia’s free, effective online lessons that they can use at home to supplement their learning at school.

The lessons keep students engaged and excited, and have resulted in significant learning gains in controlled trials with low-income students in India. Oppia can respond intelligently to the student, providing personalised feedback even when you're not available.

Oppia was created by a global community of volunteers who aim to provide every student with access to effective and affordable education. Join our community to support the project, request lessons needed by your students, and help create lessons for others. All of Oppia's lessons are free to use.

Let's work together on improving education for underserved students

If you are working to help improve education for underserved students, we'd love to work with you!

Oppia provides free, effective online lessons that you can use to scale your capacity while working together with an recognized international nonprofit. Since Oppia can provide students with intelligent feedback, it can help take some of the teaching load off the volunteer, allowing them to use their time with the students most effectively. Students can also do additional learning at home to complement the time spent with the volunteer.

Contact us to arrange a pilot with your students.

Help improve access to high-quality education for everyone

As a volunteer-led nonprofit, Oppia provides many opportunities for volunteering to improve education for students around the world and to give back to the broader community. Volunteering is a fun learning experience, and many of our volunteers have developed new skills in the course of volunteering with the project. Volunteers who are responsible and committed may also be offered leadership roles in the Oppia community.

Please see our volunteer page at OppiaFoundation.org for more details on the opportunities available. You can help as a designer, developer, researcher or lesson creator. There's a great deal of valuable work we need to do, so every additional volunteer increases the number of students we can help!
