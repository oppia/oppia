Our Mission
To help anyone learn anything they want in an effective and enjoyable way.

Oppia means "learn" in Finnish, and this site is all about learning and sharing knowledge! Oppia is a free, open-source learning platform that offers interactive learning modules that recreate the power of 1-on-1 tutoring. Our unique technology allows anyone to create these learning modules, or, as we call them, explorations, to share their knowledge in the most effective and efficient way. Since these explorations adapt to the needs and the current knowledge of learners, Oppia provides an enriched learning experience. Oppia's explorations cover a variey of subjects, like mathematics, biology, and programming, and are offered in various languages made by unique individuals like you.

The Oppia Foundation

The Oppia website and source code are supported by the Oppia Foundation, a non-profit public benefit corporation of the State of California.

The directos of the Foundation are Amit Deutsch, Jacob Davis, and Sean Lip. The Foundation's bylaws and minutes are available to read. If you wish to contact the Foundation, please email: admin@oppia.org
