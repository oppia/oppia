Privacy Policy

Oppia's mission is to make it easy for anyone to learn anything they want to in an effective and enjoyable way. As part of achieving that mission, Oppia ("we" or "us") collects some information from its users to deliver an interactive and engaging experience. This Privacy Notice outlines our privacy policy for how Oppia collects, uses, and protects information we collect from users of the Oppia website (https://www.oppia.org) (the "Site"). In addition to the website, the Oppia codebase may be downloaded and hosted independently; this scenario is not covered by this Privacy Policy.

What information does Oppia collect?

Oppia is designed to be open and free, which means that you do not have to register or create an account to use the site content. Remember, you never have to share your personal information if you simply wish to browse public content. Please note that Oppia collects the following information from users of the Site:

  • any information collected or generated by your use of the content, such as:
    • your answers to Explorations
    • when you begin and end an Exploration
    • what pages you visit while visiting the Site
    • any playthrough-specific customizations to the Exploration (such as randomly generated parameters)
    • any contributions you make to the Site (such as feedback on Explorations, edits to Explorations, and Explorations you create)
  • certain information about your system, such as:
    • the IP address of the computer used to access the Site
    • what browser or device you are using to visit the Site
    • what operating system you are using
    • the dates and times of your visit to the Site
    • what page you navigated to the Site from
    • what page you navigate to when you leave the Site

In addition, if you do choose to register with the Site, Oppia also collects any information you submit to us, including the following sorts of personally identifiable information:

  • your email address
  • your user name
  • your photo (if you choose to upload one)
  • any other information you submit to us during your profile registration process or on any forms or other input fields on the Site

Please note that data we collect about you during playthrough of an Exploration is tied to a randomly-generated session identifier which is generated on-the-fly at the start of the playthrough, and is not associated with any individual user identifier.

Additionally, since Oppia is built on Google App Engine, basic per-request logging is enabled. The relevant aspects of Google’s privacy policy apply to all users of Oppia. Oppia does not use advanced logging capabilities beyond the default configuration.

How does Oppia use the information it collects?

Oppia uses information about Exploration playthroughs to guide the learning experience, to evaluate the effectiveness of contributions, to improve our understanding of the learning process and to otherwise improve the effectiveness of our offerings.

We may publish or otherwise publicize results from this process, but, unless otherwise permitted under this Privacy Policy, those publications or public disclosures will not include personally identifiable information.

For logged-in users, Oppia uses information about exploration answers and completions to help users keep track of their progress, and to suggest new explorations for them to try. Oppia also keeps track of the edits, feedback and other contributions that logged-in users have made to site content, for attribution purposes. These records are world-viewable.

Google App Engine, the service that Oppia runs on top of, uses cookies. Cookies are small text files with a string of alphanumeric characters that keep track of user activities. In the case of Oppia, cookies help us determine when users are logged in and thereby track user activity. If you want to modify your cookie settings, please refer to your browser settings.

Oppia also uses other third party services like Google Analytics to better understand how our users use our website. We allow third party services like Google Analytics to use cookies and other technologies to collect this information. This information is collected directly by these third party services. For more information about these services, please refer to the privacy notice of these services.

If, during your visit to our Site, you do not want information collected through the use of cookies, you may change the settings on your browser to either automatically decline cookies, or to give you the choice of declining or accepting the cookies from a particular site on a case-by-case basis, or, in some instances, to automatically delete cookies upon exiting the browser. If you choose not to accept cookies from our Site, you may experience some reduced functionality or other inconvenience while accessing the Site.

How is your information shared or transferred?

We do not share any of the personally identifiable information we collect with third parties. We may share or transfer such information we collect to our third-party service providers (like Google Analytics), but only for purposes of their providing services to us and never for their own purposes. However, in rare instances, it may be necessary for us to disclose your information. Because of this, we reserve the right to disclose information we collect: (a) to comply with applicable legal requirements (for example, responding to subpoenas); (b) to respond to requests from public and government authorities; (c) to enforce our Terms of Use; (d) to protect our, your, and/or third parties’ rights, privacy, safety or property; and (e) to allow us to pursue available remedies or limit the damages that we may sustain.

Please note that certain information is publicly accessible on the site, such as: users' bios, usernames, and profile pictures.

All content created on Oppia is licensed under CC-BY-SA 4.0.

How do we store and protect your personal information?

Oppia is committed to protecting your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. Unfortunately, we cannot guarantee complete security and a breach could occur due to factors beyond our control. In the event of a breach, we will take reasonable steps to notify affected users. If you have reason to believe that your data has been compromised or your use of the Site is no longer secure, please immediately notify us of the problem by contacting us at the following email address: admin@oppia.org

Updating your personal information

Oppia is designed to be open and free, which means that you do not have to register or create an account to use the site content. Remember, you never have to share your personal information if you simply wish to browse public content.

If you register and provide Oppia with personal information, you may update, correct, or delete it at any time by reviewing your profile information and preferences on your account settings page. The only exception to this rule is that we cannot allow users to change their email address or username.

Child users

Oppia does not target or exclude specific user segments. If you are under 13, we do not want your personal information and you should not provide it to us. If you are a legal guardian and believe that your child who is under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us to have your child's information removed.

International visitors

Oppia is built on services whose computers may be in various countries, which means that your information might end up on a computer in a country other than where you reside. By giving us information, you consent to this kind of transfer of your information. Oppia cannot provide data protection guarantees for any particular region.

Links to other sites

Our Site and content may feature links to third party websites that offer goods, services or information. When you click on one of these links, you will be leaving our Site and will no longer be subject to this Policy. We are not responsible for the information collection practices of the other websites that you visit and urge you to review their privacy policies before you provide them with any personally identifiable information. Third party sites or services may collect and use information about you in a way that is different from this Policy.

Changes and updates to this Privacy Policy

Oppia will continue to evolve and we would like to ensure that our Privacy Policy reflects our practices. Any changes to this Policy will become effective immediately upon posting of the Policy on the Site. Your continued use of the Site following these changes constitutes your agreement to the revised Policy.

Contacting Oppia

If you have questions about this Privacy Notice or would like to provide feedback, please send an e-mail to admin@oppia.org.
